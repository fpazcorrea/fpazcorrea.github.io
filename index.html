About Me

I am a Chilean Astronomy & Astrophysics Ph.D. student at the University of Chicago. I am mainly interested on studying the dynamical evolution of exoplanetary systems and their architectures. I am also interested in the role that different planet interior structures and potential exo-moons could take in this broad scenario.
